Lucerna Studios

Engaging educational games that bring families together.

What we do

Lucerna studios makes Virtual Reality (VR) educational games for homeschool families. Our main priority is creating a tool that doesn't only engage kids but also help parents save time. Experiencing new technology and fun learning games will have your family looking forward to a new adventure every lesson.

Kinesthetic Learning

Lucerna uses Virtual Reality as a Kinesthetic learning tool. Carrying out physical activities is proven to be one of the most effective ways of learning and an inmense help with dyslexia and ADD.

Free up some personal time

Our games have built-in lesson plans and auto grading that will save you time so you can focus on building a loving family.

Find the gamer in you

Kids live in a world where technology is an essential part of life. Be part of there lives by playing video games together and creating meaningful experiences.
