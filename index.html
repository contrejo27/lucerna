What we do

Lucerna studios makes Virtual Reality (VR) educational games for schools. Our priority is creating a tool that doesn't only engage students but is also teacher friendly. Since VR is a new technology, there are plenty of ways it can be implemented. From phone VR games that students can take home to the more stationary Oculus rift, which is the latest in immersive technology. We will guide you to ensure VR best fits your school needs.

Tactile Learning

Lucerna uses Virtual Reality as a tactile learning tool. Carrying out physical activities is proven to be one of the most effective ways of learning.

Teach to the standard

Our games have built-in lesson plans that hit common core standards along with customizable content

Engaging Gameplay

Bring something new to the classroom. Seeing new technology and fun games will have your students looking forward to a new adventure every lesson.
